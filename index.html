About Blake

Hey! My name is Blake Zimmerman and I am a Co-Founder at 4D Surgical where we are working to build a new paradigm in imge-guided procedures. Previously, I contributed through postdoctoral research at the Utah Center for Advanced Imaging Research (UCAIR) and the Scientific Computing and Imaging (SCI) Institute. I am constantly intrigued by new problems and novel machine learning applications to provide user-driven solutions to these new problems.
